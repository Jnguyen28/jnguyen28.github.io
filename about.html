Sunset at Kitsilano

: Tiffany Nguyen.
I spent a few hours back in Winnipeg convincing my cousin to come on a hike with me up to Garibaldi Lake. Might have understated the difficulty of the hike a little bit. After being forced to go to a chiropractor and turning a few of his toes black (his fault for wearing the wrong shoes), it's safe to say that he won't be coming on any hikes like that one again any time soon. This sunset was the aftermath of it all - a perfect way to end the day.
